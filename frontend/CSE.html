About CSE Department

The Computer Science and Engineering Department at Institute Of Engineering And Technology, Khandari affilated by Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar University is committed to providing high-quality education, fostering research and innovation, and preparing students for successful careers in the field of computer science.

The major goal of the department of computer science and engineering is to be a center of excellence in education and research in the frontier areas of computer science and engineering. The department of computer and engineering (CSE) prepares for a career that is at the heart of the modern industrial revolution. Our expert faculty and advanced research infrastructure motivate aspiring professsionals to persive cse at our college and build bright carrier in almost any field in engineering and science from IT.

Vision Of The CSE Department

To become a center of excellence in Computer Science & Engineering to address the societal, rural, and global industry challenges by applying knowledge, innovative methods, and research skills.

Mission Of The Department

To provide the state-of-the-art infrastructure and employ competent & committed human resources for carrying out teaching, research, and innovation.

Developing strong foundations in core areas of computer science and engineering by subsuming theory with extensive practical training and exposure to industry.

Developingskilled professionalsfor Industry and/or R&D organizations.

Developing value based socially committed professionalism for the holistic development.
