Welcome to Club-Nest IET Khandari

Greetings and welcome to Club-Nest, the vibrant hub of innovation and creativity within the IET Khandari community! As an integral part of the Institution of Engineering and Technology, Khandari Chapter, Club-Nest is where passion meets technology, and ideas take flight.

What We Offer

Innovation Workshops: Engage in hands-on workshops and sessions that stimulate your creativity and expose you to cutting-edge technologies.

Tech Talks and Seminars: Stay informed and inspired through insightful talks by industry experts and professionals in various technological domains.

Project Collaborations: Join forces with like-minded peers to work on exciting projects, applying theoretical knowledge to real-world applications.

Networking Opportunities: Connect with industry professionals, alumni, and fellow students, creating valuable networks that extend beyond the academic realm.

Our Vision

Club-Nest envisions a future where every student is empowered to think critically, innovate fearlessly, and contribute meaningfully to the world of technology. We strive to create an inclusive community that nurtures talent, celebrates diversity, and prepares students for success in their engineering careers.
