AIIOT Club


AI & IoT clubs empower members to delve into the realms of artificial intelligence and the Internet of Things. Participants gain practical experience, fostering skills in coding, data analytics, and device connectivity. Through collaborative projects, members explore innovative solutions, preparing for careers in tech. These clubs provide a platform for networking, staying updated on industry trends, and contributing to the transformative fields of AI and IoT.

Incharge of Club : Er. Ankita Maheshwari

I C T based One week faculty development program on machine learning and AI

23 dec2019 -27 dec 2019 N.B. Hall

• Provide a foundational understanding of machine learning and AI concepts.

• Introduce faculty members to fundamental machine learning algorithms such as linear regression, logistic regression, decision trees, and k-nearest neighbors.

• Teach techniques for feature engineering and data preprocessing to enhance model performance.

• Discuss the importance of clean and well-processed data.

• Create platforms for participants to network, share experiences, and explore potential collaborations.

I C T based One week faculty development program on Natural language processing

 6 jan 2019 – 10 jan 2019 N.B. Hall

• Provide a comprehensive understanding of the fundamentals of Natural Language Processing.

• Equip faculty members with practical skills in using popular NLP tools and libraries, such as NLTK, spaCy, and TensorFlow.

• Explore the role of deep learning in NLP, covering architectures like Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM), and Transformers.

• Guide students to participate effectively in group discussions.
